Spam: as physical object

Bringing Spam to the physical world and exploring how giving online advertising a print form changes our value of it. The tool of tracking user data on the internet has become widely accessable, but the style of pay-per-click online advertising has been show as not an accurate indicator of the revenue an ad generates. This comes as the progression of "spam" transfered from printed matter to more and more digital ads the general population has become more discerning because of the large variety of options open to consumers. This is all to say the model of low cost advertisement to reach a wide audience has become so low cost it has far to little value for companies so I wanted to experiment and see if giving these ads objecthood would make them anymore appealing. For the design I wanted a simple system that borrowed from the naive visual language of email spam.

Typeset in Arial and Lo-res

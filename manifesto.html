Anti-Surveillance Manifesto

This came from a project to write our own manifesto and give it form. My manifesto centered around disrupting the main economy of digital spaces by obfuscating all of your public tweets, and Facebook posts, so the effort in gathering your information exceeds the value of the information for targeted advertisements; or renders the information useless.




I created this twitter bot to disseminate my manifesto by replying to any tweets that contained the keyworkds"NSA" or "surveillance".

It eventually was sutdown, but succeeded in tweeting over 10,000 times.


This website was a experiment to try and create something that could be resized to be readable to humans but not machines.

This site was also responsible for containing a .pdf of a zine explaining more obfuscation expiriments.

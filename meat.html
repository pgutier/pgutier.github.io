Meat Culture Info Booth

In-vitro meat, as with all new technology, poses us with the question of how exciting and how much good this technology could do. When something like this is on the horizon, I believe it is the responsibility of the designer to present the best way this tech can affect the world and give us path to move forward in a positive direction.

I made the display table and set up to discuss with people on campus what their perception of this technology is and to inform them if they were not aware of it before.
